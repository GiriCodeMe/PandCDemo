✅ NWS Official Certification
This report certifies that a Severe Thunderstorm Warning (NWS-SVR-HOU-2026-0308) was in effect for Harris County, Texas on March 8, 2026 from 2:22 PM to 3:47 PM CST. The storm produced confirmed damaging winds of 67 miles per hour and hail measuring 0.9 inches in diameter (quarter-size). These conditions are consistent with and sufficient to cause significant roof damage to residential structures, including shingle displacement, roof deck exposure, and gutter damage. This document is suitable for use in insurance claims, legal proceedings, and property damage assessments.
